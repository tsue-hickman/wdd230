Directory

For information on how to reach us or businesses in our chamber, here is our contact information. The directory page will be filled with information about how to contact different businesses within the chamber or how to contact the board members of the actual chamber. It will also list the local businesses, corporations, and organizations who take part in the chamber of commerce here in Omaha.

Join Page

This is where a business, corporation, or organization who desires to join the chamber will input their information to be considered for a position.

This is where the responsibilites and benefits of joining our chamber will be displayed. There are different levels of membership, the highest being gold, middle being silver, and the base line being non-profit. Each level comes with different benefits and roles in which one plays in the community and should be joined based on the type of business or business goals in which an organization, corporation, or business wants to achieve. Different perks/ opportuniites may include sponsorships, publications, advertising, directory listings,trainings, social media work, luncheons, etc.
